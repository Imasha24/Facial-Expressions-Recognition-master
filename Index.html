Babies Face Expression Detection System

Our system detects and classifies the expressions of your baby's face in real time. Upload your baby's photo and get instant feedback on their expressions.

How to Use

To use our system, simply follow these steps:

  1. Sign up for an account or log in to your existing account.
  2. Upload a photo of your baby's face.
  3. Click the "Analyze" button to detect and classify your baby's facial expressions.
  4. View the results and use the feedback to better understand your baby's emotions.

About Us

We are a team of developers and researchers committed to advancing the understanding of baby facial expressions. Our system is based on cutting-edge machine learning algorithms and is constantly being updated with new features and improvements.
